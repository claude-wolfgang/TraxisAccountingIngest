Traxis Accounting Ingest is a private, internal application developed by and for Traxis Manufacturing, Inc. It is not publicly available and has no external users.

Intended use

This application is designed to assist Traxis staff with processing incoming accounting documents (vendor invoices, packing slips, purchase orders) by classifying them with AI and routing them to QuickBooks Online or ProShop ERP.

Authorization

By connecting your QuickBooks Online company to this application, you authorize it to create Bills, query Vendors and Accounts, and upload PDF attachments on behalf of the connected company.

Disconnecting

You may disconnect this application from your QuickBooks company at any time by:

  1. Visiting QuickBooks Online → Settings → Manage connected apps
  2. Selecting Traxis Accounting Ingest and clicking Disconnect

Upon disconnection, the application will no longer be able to access your QuickBooks data. Locally stored queue records and extracted data will remain on Traxis machines until manually deleted.

Limitation of liability

This application is provided as-is for internal Traxis use. Traxis Manufacturing makes no warranties regarding the accuracy of AI-extracted data. All records are subject to human review before being pushed to QuickBooks Online.
