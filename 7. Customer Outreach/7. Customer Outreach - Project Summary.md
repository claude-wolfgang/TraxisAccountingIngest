# 7. Customer Outreach - Project Summary

## Objective

Reduce customer concentration risk by systematically acquiring new customers while leveraging existing capabilities and local proximity to potential clients.

---

The shop currently has over-dependence on one major customer, creating concentration risk that threatens business stability. Diversification through targeted customer acquisition is essential for long-term health.

- **Existing Customers:**
